🎒 What to Pack for Uruguay

  1. Essentials:
    • Rain jacket
    • Durable shoes
    • Light sweaters for windy days
  2. Spanish Scriptures
  3. Comfortable proselyting clothes

🍽️ Food in Uruguay

Uruguay has a rich food culture! Here are a few foods you'll love:

  • Chivito: A steak sandwich with everything
  • Empanadas: Stuffed pastries with meat or cheese
  • Alfajores: Dulce de leche cookies

☀️ Weather

Uruguay has four seasons but is generally mild:

  • Summer: December to February, warm and humid
  • Winter: June to August, chilly but not extreme
  • Expect wind all year, especially in Montevideo

📘 Mission Tips

Some helpful things for those preparing to serve in Uruguay:

  • Practice understanding Uruguayan Spanish (they say "vos")
  • Bring extra garments—they may be hard to replace
  • Pack bug spray and sunscreen!
